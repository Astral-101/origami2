INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CAMEL

  • Camels ears are furry
  • Camel's can move easily across the sand because of their specially designed feet
  • When camels find water, they drink as much as possible

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT CHAMELEON

  • Chameleons can change colors in order to escape predators
  • Chameleons like to eat insects and other small bugs.
  • Chameleons have eyes in the back of the head

INTERESTING FACTS ABOUT PANDA

  • Like domestic cats, giant pandas have vertical slits for pupils.
  • They have an extended wrist bone that they use like a thumb to help them grip food.
  • At 5 months old, giant pandas learn how to climb - sometimes practicing by climbing on their mum.
